About Simple Scholar

The goal of Simple Scholar is to provide a simple, and convenient method for you, as a busy student, to get your textbooks for the semester!

The Team

The Simple Scholar Team

Consisting of several college students who know all to well the experience of getting textbooks the hard way, the Simple Scholar team set out to solve these devastating stuggles for all future students.

Services

Services

Simple Scholar offers delivery of your textbook order directly to your dorm room. In addition, all orders are available for pickup at the MK Great Hall within 30 minutes of placing your order.

 This website is intended to act as an additional tool for an existing college class registration system, to simulate this, we ask all users to select a major when they register for our website. The major you choose will then be used to randomly generate a mock class schedule for you. This mock schedule is a key element in order to demonstrate the true functionality of our website.

 After registering for our website, you will be shown your generated schedule, and given the option to regenerate your schedule if you wish. Once you confirm your schedule, you will be taken to the book selection page. Each book on this page is gathered according to the textbook needs of the classes in your schedule. Here you can choose to remove any books which you may already own, or have chosen to purchase elsewhere.

 Once you finish choosing which textbooks to order, you will be brought to the checkout page. On this page you will be given the option to rent or buy both new or used textbooks. After choosing your textbook purchasing preferences, you will be asked if you would like to have your order either picked up or delivered. Once you confirm your order you will move one to the payment process after that you are done!
